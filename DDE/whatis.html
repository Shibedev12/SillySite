About DanceDanceEngine:

DanceDanceEngine, better known as DDE and formerly DDS (DanceDanceSprigalution), was made in Hack Club's Javascript-based project, Sprig.

DDE is a labor of love, made by me, Shibedev12. nobody else.

DDE is made with Sprig. It is made to be edited, changed and fixed as time progresses!

Here's a nonexhaustive list of everything that DDS can do:

  • Add Custom BackGroundMusic!
  • Adjust and make your own note patterns!
  • Customize your note sprites, color swap and more!
